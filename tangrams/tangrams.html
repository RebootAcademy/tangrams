Outline: blank 1 2 3 4 5 6

The object of the puzzle is to fit all of the shape pieces into the outlined area such that no piece overlaps another or is outside of the outline.

Choose an outline by clicking a number on the left. Choose no outline by clicking "blank."

To move the shapes, click and drag the shape pieces with your mouse.

Use the left, right, up, and down arrow keys as well as the enter, shift, slash (/), and quotation mark (') keys to rotate and flip. the shape pieces.
